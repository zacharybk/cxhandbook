The CX Handbook is a collection of practical strategies, frameworks, and playbooks for building world-class customer experience teams.

## What You'll Find

### Playbooks
Step-by-step guides for common CX challenges and processes. From product launches to support workflows, these frameworks can be implemented today.

### Customer Feedback
Strategies for collecting, analyzing, and acting on customer feedback. Learn how to measure Customer Effort Score and make the case for AI in your CX organization.

### Metrics
Key metrics and KPIs for measuring CX success. Understand what to measure and how to improve.

### Blog
Thoughts, insights, and experiences from years of building CX teams at high-growth companies.

## About

This handbook is written by [Zach Bouzan-Kaloustian](https://www.linkedin.com/in/zacharybk), drawing from experience building and scaling customer experience teams.
